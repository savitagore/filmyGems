Rashmika Mandanna (born 5 April 1996) is an Indian actress who works in Telugu, Kannada, Hindi and Tamil films. She is the recipient of four SIIMA Awards and a Filmfare Award. She featured in the 30 Under 30 list by Forbes India in 2024. Rashmika made her acting debut with the Kannada film Kirik Party (2016), and found further success in the action picture Anjani Putra and romance Chamak (both 2017). She had her first Telugu film release in the romantic comedy Chalo (2018).
